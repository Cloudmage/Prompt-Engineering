3. Opportunity Matching
 - Match findings against {ADD IN SITES} information to list what can be offer against each company

- Craft the data into a Business Value Framework

- Maintain objectivity and balance

5. Quality Control
- Verify all facts and attributes
- Add context where necessary
- Include future implications.
